# of compiled C-extension packages (pyarrow, numpy, pydantic-core used by
# supabase/postgrest) is far less tested and more prone to exactly the kind
# of silent segfault this app was hitting. On 3.11, a mainstream, far more
# battle-tested range is both sufficient and safer.
# ------------------------------------------------------------------------------
